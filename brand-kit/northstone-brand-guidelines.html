01 · The brand

Real estate solutions built on trust

NorthStone Property buys residential properties directly from Canadian homeowners. The brand is calm, credible, and institutional — closer to a boutique investment firm than a "we buy houses" operator. Every touchpoint should feel unhurried, transparent, and premium.

02 · Logo

Logo system

The mark interlocks an N with a rooftop and rising tower — north, home, and growth in one form. The two-tone wordmark splits NorthStone between Midnight Navy and Stone.

Primary — white and light (Mist) backgrounds. Default in all applications.
Reverse — Midnight Navy and photography. Navy elements switch to white; Stone is retained.
Mark only — favicons, avatars, app icons, and small placements where the wordmark would be illegible.
Navy tile — social profiles and app-icon contexts.

Clearspace & minimums

  • Keep clearspace equal to the height of the wordmark's "N" on all sides.
  • Minimum width: 140 px digital / 32 mm print for the full lockup; use the mark below that.
  • Place only on white, Mist, Midnight Navy, or calm photography with a navy scrim.

Never

  • Recolour the mark, add gradients, outlines, or drop shadows.
  • Stretch, rotate, or rearrange the mark and wordmark.
  • Set the logo on busy imagery or mid-contrast colours.
  • Pair it with urgency devices — starbursts, countdowns, "CASH NOW" flashes.
03 · Colour

Palette

Two brand colours sampled directly from the logo, supported by a quiet set of functional neutrals. Navy leads; Stone accents; everything else stays out of the way.

Core

Midnight Navy#081E34 · rgb(8,30,52)Headers, footers, headings, reverse surfaces. The brand anchor.
Stone#736C63 · rgb(115,108,99)Eyebrows, hairlines, secondary accents on light backgrounds.
Action Navy#14375E · rgb(20,55,94)Buttons and links — a lighter navy so interactive elements read against Midnight.

Supporting

Ink Soft#11294AHover states and layering on Midnight Navy.
Stone Light#B8B1A7Stone's stand-in on dark surfaces (accessible contrast).
Navy Tint#E9EEF5Highlight chips, success surfaces, selected states.
Mist#F4F6F8Alternate section background.
Body#2A3745Body copy on light backgrounds.
Muted#5B6B7CCaptions, metadata, secondary text.
Accessibility

Midnight Navy and Action Navy both exceed WCAG AAA on white. Stone passes AA for normal text on white (5.4:1). On navy surfaces, always substitute Stone Light for Stone and use white at 60–80% opacity for secondary copy.

06 · Voice

Voice & tone

Say

  • "A written offer within 24 hours — take the time you need."
  • "No fees, no commissions, no repairs."
  • "Many homeowners use our offer simply as a benchmark."
  • "Have your lawyer review everything — we encourage it."

Never say

  • "CASH NOW!!!" — no all-caps urgency, no exclamation stacking.
  • "This offer expires in 48 hours." — we don't pressure.
  • "Top dollar / highest price guaranteed" — we're honest that convenience has a trade-off.
  • Jargon without translation — every legal term gets a plain-language explanation.
